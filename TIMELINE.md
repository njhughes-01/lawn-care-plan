# Lawn Care Timeline & Reminders

## Key Dates for 2026

### ✅ March 28 (DONE)
- Initial plan created
- Fact-check completed against CSU Extension + Aurora Water restrictions

### April - May: Maintenance Phase
- Water 2x/week (Aurora max)
- Mow at 3-4 inches
- No fertilizer, no seeding
- Let herbicides work

### May (When Grass Greens Up)
**ACTION REQUIRED:** Identify existing grass type
- Take close-up photos of grass blades (blade tip shape)
- Take overall lawn texture photos
- Send to Aura for ID
- See `grass-identification-guide.md` for what to look for

### June - July: Survival Mode
- Water 2x/week (deep watering)
- Mow at 3-4 inches
- Accept lawn will look stressed - this is normal

### 🚨 July 15: CRITICAL DECISION POINT
**ACTION REQUIRED:** Check Aurora Water restrictions
- Visit aurorawater.org or call 303-739-7195
- Question: "Will 2026 fall water restrictions allow daily watering for new grass seed germination?"
- If YES → Proceed to August prep
- If NO → Postpone renovation to 2027

### August 1-10: Seed Ordering Window (IF RESTRICTIONS ALLOW)
**ACTION REQUIRED:** Order seed from local dealer
- Arkansas Valley Seed: (970) 384-8211, avseeds.com
- Pawnee Buttes Seed: pawneebuttesseed.com
- Southwest Seed: southwestseed.com
- Order: 15-20 lbs fine fescue/bluegrass blend (drought-tolerant cultivars)

### August 5-10: Apply for Aurora Lawn & Seed Permit
**ACTION REQUIRED:** Get permit BEFORE starting work
- Cost: $42.75 (residential)
- Apply online: devportal.auroragov.org
- Grants temporary exemption from watering restrictions
- Required for inspection

### August 10-15: Order Bulk Compost
**ACTION REQUIRED:** Schedule delivery
- Front yard: ~4.4 cubic yards
- Backyard patches: ~14 cubic yards
- Total: ~18-20 cubic yards
- Use Aurora-approved supplier (A-1 Organics, Alpine Waste, etc.)

### August 15-20: Front Yard Renovation
**EXECUTION WINDOW:**
1. Reserve core aerator rental
2. Mark sprinkler heads
3. Aerate heavily (multiple passes)
4. Apply compost (4 cu yd per 1,000 sq ft, rototilled 6" deep)
5. Schedule Aurora inspection (before seeding)
6. Apply seed after inspection approval
7. Daily watering for 2-3 weeks

### August 20-25: Backyard Patches
**EXECUTION WINDOW:**
- Same process as front yard, but only worst 3,000-4,000 sq ft

### September 1-20: Latest Seeding Window
- Soil temps drop after mid-September
- New seed may not establish if planted too late

### September-October: Germination & Establishment
- Daily light watering for 2-3 weeks post-seeding
- Transition to deep watering 2x/week after establishment
- First mow when grass reaches 3-4 inches
