## Buffalograss Question (Father-in-law's suggestion)

**Verdict:** Not viable for this property

**Pros:**
- Most drought-tolerant lawn grass
- Lowest water/maintenance needs

**Cons (deal-breakers):**
- Only green May-Sept (brown 8 months/year)
- No shade tolerance
- **Poor traffic tolerance** (dogs + kids = failure)
- Cannot mix with cool-season grasses (requires full lawn kill)

**Possible future:** Consider for low-traffic sunny backyard zone as xeriscape
